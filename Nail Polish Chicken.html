Nail Polish Chicken

Ingredients List

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless chicken thighs
  • 2 large red onions
  • 1 1/2 inch stick of ginger
  • 10 cloves garlic
  • 5 fresh Thai green chillies
  • 4 large or 6 medium yellow potatoes
  • Mustard Oil
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Peel and dice onions. Remove to side.
  2. Peel and finely chop ginger, garlic, and chillies. Add to onions.
  3. Dry chicken thighs and place in large bowl. Add the onion, ginger, garlic, and chillies. Cover to marinate for one hour.
  4. Peel and cut potatoes to medium-to-large sized pieces. Wash.
  5. Heat a pot on medium-high, and add enough mustard oil to coat the bottom (about 1-3 tsp).
  6. Add in potatoes, and shallow fry until brown. Remove onto a plate lined with paper towel, salt, and mix vigorously.
  7. Add chicken and marinade into the pot. Stir vigorously, but carefully, as to not break the chicken, so that nothing sits at the bottom for too long.
  8. When water starts to come out, lower heat to medium-low, add the potatoes back in, and cover with a lid. Simmer for 45-60 minutes.
  9. When potatoes are tender, transfer to a bowl and serve!

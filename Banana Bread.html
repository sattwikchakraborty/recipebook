Banana Bread

Ingredients List

Wet Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup of softened unsalted butter (1 stick)
  • 75g white sugar
  • 2 eggs at room temperature
  • 2 tbsp plain yogurt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Dry Ingredients

  • 250g all-purpose-flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon powder
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg powder

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350oF and spray a bread pan with Canola Spray
  2. Peel the bananas into a medium-sized bowl. Mash with a firk until wet and lumpy.
  3. In a bowl, sift together the dry ingredients
  4. In a stand mixer set at medium speed, cream the butter until stuck to the sides.
  5. Add in the sugar, increase the speed to medium-high and cream until light and fluffy. The sugar will not be fully dissolved.
  6. Add in the eggs, one at a time, scraping the sides of the bowl and making sure each egg is fully incorporated before adding the next.
  7. Add in the yogurt and vanilla extract. Mix until combined. Remove from stand mixer.
  8. With a silicon spoon, beat in the mashed bananas and all juices from the bowl.
  9. Fold in the dry ingredients and lightly mix until just combined.
  10. Add into bread pan and bake for 45-55 minutes, until a skewer comes out completely dry.
  11. Allow to cool in pan for 5 minutes, then serve!
